Gallery

Canada

An unexplored wilderness of majestic beauty and diverse wildlife. Wander through this very magical place and witness its rich natural beauty. Discover the mountains, the rivers, the valleys, and find out the secrets of flora and fauna in breathtaking natural surroundings. Stunning vistas of lakes and bays will leave you in admiration.

China

A land of sprawling cities and wild nature, rolling hills, towering mountains, tall trees, rumbling rivers and sunny skies. A land where there are myriad types of rice cakes and mugs of baijiu to be had, where scenic walkways are tucked away. Where the mountains and the ancient landscapes complement each other with a flawless harmony. Where you can escape your world for days on end with a simple trip to visit the Kyaiktang Waterfall or the Ligu Cliffs.

Egypt

An ancient land of mysteries, Sphinxes, pyramids and a great fallen civilisation. A civilisation that we are still trying to understand but could never fully comprehend. Not just its extraordinary natural beauty but the legends of its great pharaohs, the wonders of its monuments. To discover its myths, legends, and hidden treasures would hold more fascination for than even the most elaborate of modern discovery sites.

Germany

From the modern metropolitan hubs to the traditions of the old-time towns. The stunning architecture, the history, all serve as a wonderful window into the German culturescape. Or scale the Bavarian mountains for a more rustic experience. The renowned beauty of the Bavarian mountains is unbelievable, see the incredible alpine meadows, the limestone and granite cliffs of the Ilmen valley or the small, seemingly miniature Alpine villages situated below the elevated areas of the Alps.

India

India, a sprawling land of many cultures, deep spirituality and abundant flavourful food. Exploring this sub-continent and its amazing civilization can certainly be daunting, but there are no parallels. Experience every corner of this country, across different terrains and through changing cuisines, embracing the bright colors and brighter people.This place always has more to offer the adventurous soul.

Ireland

A lush green land with dramatic vistas of gloomy clouds, rolling mountains, sand and sea, Kerry is a gem. The freedom to wander around the lovely countryside makes it one of the most pleasant places to visit in Ireland. This beauty is located in south-east Ireland. It is a marvel to see the brilliant sunset as it blazes below the western horizon. There is the verdant countryside and relaxed, peaceful feeling that the rural setting of Kerry creates. This beauty is located in south-east Ireland.

Italy

In the past, the center of culture for a great civilisation, now a center of food and fashion. Italians, with their innovative culture and lifestyles, are a living memory of the Roman empire. Tuscany's beauty, , richness and diversity remain rooted in tradition. In regions like Calabria,, Rimini and Massa Speranza, Roman ruins are still found. It can be tempting to imagine a world of once rich, vibrant Roman civilization

Japan

Japan's beauty is a fine delicacy. In autumn and winter, such beautiful light can be seen. The same scenes changing, season by season, year after year. Nature's wonderful light that shines throughout the month of December when the sun is at its zenith. The famous snowing day in winter, the closed gap during the changing of the seasons or the beautiful, delicate sea of cherry blossom trees, symbolize the sacred, mystical and universal beauty that is truly Japanese beauty.

Kenya

Take a safari in the picturesque saharan grasslands of Kenya. Everywhere are miles of grass, woodlands and sand that look like a bright, lush postcard. In the rare moments that the rains seem to dry up, a moment like now, we can expect large stretches of the savannah to come alive. In the early morning, when the sun hits the shadows of trees, insects begin to fly and the sky glows with the brightest yellow and green. A romantic way of life to take full advantage of nature in a way that brings deep contentment, tranquility and peace.

Mexico

Vivid colors and vibrant people, the splendor will charm you and wake you up from beautiful dreams. With beautiful dancers, theatrical entertainment and authentic traditional folk dances, there is nothing more to do here other than relax and enjoy your time here. From vibrant nightlife and galleries to dazzling skyscrapers and mesmerizing culture. Filled with stunning monuments, extraordinary food, superb cuisine and fun culture, live life to the fullest in Mexico.

Morocco

Pungent spices and trinkets line the market stalls of Marrakesh in Morocco. The desert oasis is home to an ancient network of fabled treasures. The desert sands blow with seductive currents of musk, vanilla and aromatic herbs. In the months between July and December, the air is rife with perfume. Between the wet desert heat and the expanse of wild flowers, the days are hot and the nights cold. The Southern summer is the most romantic. Experience a little bit of paradise in the desert.

United States of America

It is in the United States that thousands of people, factories, offices, make their greatest efforts to realise their dreams. Towering skyscrapers cover entire skylines. Get lost in the wilderness of the north-west or the frontier towns of the south. Watch whales in the vast seas of the west or visit the river valleys of the south. From the Great Basin in the west to the Gulf of Mexico in the south to the Arctic in the north, over the whole length of the United States, from New York to Seattle, from sea to shining sea, a labyrinth of cities, farms, streets, hills and forests exists: one continuous, constant, chaotic world.

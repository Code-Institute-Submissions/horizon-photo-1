Morocco

Morocco was full of bright colors, pungent spices, scorching heat and the hustle and bustle of the marketplace. I loved seeing the vendors and doing business, although I would try and negotiate every last sale to get that best price. For those of you who are on a budget and haven't yet travelled here, I can tell you there are a lot of deals to be found in the market. Pick out something to buy, find the best prices, and find your way home! For those of you interested in reading, the main city to visit for nature is Essaouira, in the Marrakech region. Germany

I explored the views, high in the fesh air of and the sunshine of the Bavarian mountains. One of the most wonderful moments of my life was when we were riding through the Bavarian Alps. As we arrived at a crossroads in a steep, undulating valley, I realized my long dream of a historical pilgrimage to the Alps, and discovered a green valley at its base lined with stone groves. I continued on in the same direction as I had come, but after arriving at a small little village, I suddenly found that I had been cut off from the group. Some of the people who were passing by spoke my language, but none of them understood my reasons in coming here.

Japan

Tokyo is a city filled with dynamic contrasts. Tradition clashes with modernity, raucous noise is meters away from serene peace, and ancient temples reflect new-wave architecture. Discover what it means to live within a maze of walls and arcs, the vibrant environment of Tokyo, the old brick and red cement shell of the old city, or the greenery and lush landscape of the parks. Whether your passion is architecture, culture, nature or spirituality, Tokyo has something for you. Planning to visit Tokyo soon? Ireland

A lush green land with dramatic vistas of gloomy clouds, rolling mountains, sand and sea, Kerry is a gem. The freedom to wander around the lovely countryside makes it one of the most pleasant places to visit in Ireland. This beauty is located in south-east Ireland. It is a marvel to see the brilliant sunset as it blazes below the western horizon. There is the verdant countryside and relaxed, peaceful feeling that the rural setting of Kerry creates.
